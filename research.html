Undergraduate Thesis

Ca–Fe Oxide on Carbon Catalyst for Biodiesel Production

Institution: Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)
Department: Chemical Engineering
Supervisor: Dr. Kazi Bayzid Kabir


This undergraduate thesis investigated the development of a heterogeneous Ca–Fe oxide catalyst supported on carbon for biodiesel production through transesterification. The study focused on catalyst synthesis, laboratory experimentation, catalyst performance evaluation, and optimization of reaction conditions to improve biodiesel yield and catalytic efficiency.

Research Contributions

  • Developed heterogeneous Ca–Fe oxide supported carbon catalyst.
  • Synthesized catalyst through laboratory-scale preparation techniques.
  • Performed biodiesel production using transesterification experiments.
  • Evaluated catalyst activity under varying reaction conditions.
  • Collected and analyzed experimental data.
  • Optimized catalyst performance and biodiesel yield.
  • Maintained laboratory safety protocols and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
